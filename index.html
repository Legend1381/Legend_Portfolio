About me

Hi, I’m Mostafa, a creative Graphic Designer, UI/UX Designer, and Front-End Developer From Mashhad, Iran. I enjoy Turning ideas into visually compelling and user-friendly digital experiences.

I’m a multidisciplinary creative specializing in graphic design, UI/UX, and front-end development. With over 5 years of professional experience in graphic design, I’ve crafted logos, brand identities, posters, catalogs, and various marketing materials, always blending creativity with strategic thinking.
I’m highly skilled in Photoshop and Illustrator, having started my design journey at just 12 years old. In the past 2 years, I’ve expanded into UI/UX design and front-end development, focusing on creating clean, intuitive websites. I work confidently with Figma, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like Vue, Vuetify, and Tailwind.
